Car Loan

What is it?

This loan is a convenient way to finance a new or used vehicle, allowing you to borrow up to 100% of the cost with fixed payment amounts. It is available for personal and business use, and covers electric and gas cars up to 10 years old. The loan term ranges from 12 to 84 months with no down payment required.

You can choose a payment frequency that suits your needs, from a weekly to a monthly basis. Additionally, you can apply for special rates and plans in collaboration with our dealership partners. Click here for more information.

If you need more information about car loans, visit our branches or call us to assist you with your request. Alternatively, you can start the application process online and follow the steps. You can also apply directly through your local car dealership.

Information

Benefits

  • Get pre-qualified in minutes.

  • Choose the payment method that best suits your needs. Visit our branches or pay through our online platforms (Mobile App, website, or Aval Pay Center).

  • Make extra payments without penalty.

  • You can apply for the loan alone or with a family member.

  • You can access three of our partners to quote your car insurance with special offers.

  • Complete your loan request online in a few minutes.

  • No down payment is required.

Security

  • For your safety, our branches only accept cash at designated clerk stations.

  • Ensure your personal information, including your home address, email, and phone number, is up to date.

  • Consider setting up a direct deposit from your savings or checking account to repay your loan.

  • Never disclose your card number or passwords to anyone or any website other than Banco de Bogotá.

Requirements

  • Be at least 18 years of age.

  • Provide proof of employment, including a recent pay stub and employment letter.

  • For self-employed individuals, a minimum of 1 year of experience in the relevant field is required.

  • Ensure your credit score is up to date.
